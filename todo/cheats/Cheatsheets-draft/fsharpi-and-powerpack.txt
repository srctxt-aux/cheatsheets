Among other features the *FSharp Powerpack* contains a Ocaml compatibility for functions like `print_int`, or `print_string`, here is how it can be referenced in fsharpi:

    fsharpi
    #r "FSharp.PowerPack.Compatibility.dll";;
    #nowarn "62";;
